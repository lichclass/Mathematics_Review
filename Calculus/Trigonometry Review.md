### ***Trigonometric Functions***

**General functions**
$$
\begin{gather*}
\sin{\theta}=\frac{y}{r} \hspace{1cm} \csc{\theta}=\frac{r}{y} \\
\cos{\theta}=\frac{x}{r} \hspace{1cm} \csc{\theta}=\frac{r}{x} \\
\tan{\theta}=\frac{y}{x} \hspace{1cm} \cot{\theta}=\frac{x}{y}
\end{gather*}
$$

### ***Trigonometry Identities***

***Elementary identities***
$$
\begin{gather*}
\csc{\theta} = \frac{1}{\sin{\theta}} \hspace{2cm}
\sec{\theta} = \frac{1}{\cos{\theta}} \hspace{2cm}
\cot{\theta} = \frac{1}{\tan{\theta}} \\
\tan{\theta} = \frac{\sin{\theta}}{\cos{\theta}} \hspace{2cm}
\cot{\theta} = \frac{\cos{\theta}}{\sin{\theta}}
\end{gather*}
$$
***More Identities***
$$
\sin^2{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}=1 \\
$$
$$
\tan^2{\theta}+1=\sec^2{\theta}
$$
$$
1+\cot^2{\theta}=\csc^2{\theta}
$$
$$
\begin{gather*}
\sin{(-\theta)}=-\sin{\theta} \\
\cos{(-\theta)}=-\cos{\theta}
\end{gather*}
$$
$$
\sin{(\theta+2\pi)}=\sin{\theta} \hspace{2cm} \cos{(\theta+2\pi)}=\cos{\theta}
$$
***Addition and Subtraction formulas***
$$
\begin{gather*}
\sin{(x+y)} = \sin{x}\cos{y}+\cos{x}\sin{y} \\
\cos{(x+y)} = \cos{x}\cos{y}+\sin{x}\sin{y}
\end{gather*}
$$
$$
\begin{gather*}
\sin{(x-y)} = \sin{x}\cos{y}-\cos{x}\sin{y} \\
\cos{(x-y)} = \cos{x}\cos{y}-\sin{x}\sin{y}
\end{gather*}
$$
$$
\begin{gather*}
\tan{(x+y)}=\frac{\tan{x}+\tan{y}}{1-\tan{x}\tan{y}} \\
\tan{(x-y)}=\frac{\tan{x}-\tan{y}}{1-\tan{x}\tan{y}}
\end{gather*}
$$
***Double-angle formulas***
$$
\begin{gather*}
\sin{2x}=2\sin{x}\cos{x} \\
\cos{2x}=\cos^2x-\sin^2{x}
\end{gather*}
$$
$$
\begin{gather*}
\sin{2x}=2\sin{x}\cos{x} \\
\cos{2x}=\cos^2x-\sin^2{x}
\end{gather*}
$$
$$
\begin{gather*}
\cos{2x}=2\cos^2{x}-1 \\
\cos{2x}=1-2\sin^2{x}
\end{gather*}
$$
***Half-angle formulas**
$$
\begin{gather*}
\cos^2{x}=\frac{1+\cos{2x}}{2} \\
\sin^2{x}=\frac{1-\cos{2x}}{2}
\end{gather*}
$$
